G.L. Walker
Wholesale Pricing System

About

The addon allows a simple way of giving products a wholesale price, and assigning customers to a wholesale status, all with very minimal impact on core files.

Successful Installation will allow the following:

  • Customers may be switched to wholesale buyers
  • Seperate wholesale price may be entered for each product
  • Seperate weight may be entered for wholesale usage
  • Text for wholesale notes - (Ex: "Sold in lots of 4")
  • Store admin may choose whether on not to charge taxes on wholesale purchases

Installation:

The following are new files:
  • includes/modules/content/login/cm_wholesale_login_form.php
  • inludes/languages/english/modules/content/login/cm_wholesale_login_form.php
  • admin/wholesale.php
  • admin/inludes/languages/english/wholesale.php
The following are modified existing files:
  • logoff.php
  • product_info.php
  • inludes/database_tables.php
  • includes/classes/shopping_cart.php
  • inludes/functions/general.php
  • inludes/modules/product_listing.php
  • admin/customers.php
  • admin/includes/filenames.php
  • admin/includes/boxes/catalog.php
  • admin/includes/languages/english.php
  • admin/includes/languages/english/customers.php

Drop On Top Quick Install
(For unmodified Shops)

Upload all files as found in the "catalog" folder.

Manual Install

Upload new files as listed above, and found in the "catalog" folder.

Merge changes in the files marked as existing files above. You may use a file compare program, or search for:

/* WS5 Wholesale

Each change has been documented.

Activate Module

Navigate to your admin's Modules->Content.
Click "Install Module" button.
Choose "Wholesale Login Form".
Click "Install Module" button.
Configure options and save.
This will also build 2 new tables in your database, customers_to_wholesale & products_to_wholesale

Next remove your existing Login Form.

Uninstall

You may choose to remove the module to drop any configuration data, but the new database tables will remain to prevent data loss in case of accidental removal.
You basically must undo all file changes and reinstall the original "Login Form".
However, the code is very unobtrusive and would not hurt to leave in place.

Usage

Customers may be assigned as wholesale by clicking on the green status indicator as found in your admins customers.php
Wholesale prices, weight, and small notes may be made in your admin wholesale.php.
The process of entering wholesale data is eerily similar to specials.php

When a wholesale customer logs in, a wholesale session is set. This session allows the wholesale price and other options to be utilized. Wholesale prices are seen on the product info page, as well as product listing within a category.
It is up to YOU to customize the output as needed, if you want to make each area a price is presented show wholesale pricing, then you must change each area with simular changes as done in:

  • product_info.php
  • inludes/modules/product_listing.php
I see very little need to do such for customers that know they are wholesale though.
